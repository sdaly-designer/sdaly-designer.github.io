T-Mobile approached my firm, TYG, in need of training materials for its employees. As the lead designer on the project, I worked with T-Mobile’s marketing team to develop an informational/educational system that included a learning manual, activity book, PowerPoint presentations & flash cards. I based the design on T-Mobile’s established brand, but created new icons to

differentiate the system from other T-Mobile materials.
