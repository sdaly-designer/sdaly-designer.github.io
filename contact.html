FAQs

I need a logo designed for my business. How much does it cost?

Unfortunately I can’t provide flat-rate pricing for projects because the scope of two projects is never the same. The cost can vary depending on the level of involvement and schedule. Please contact me with the project details, and I’ll be happy to provide a complete estimate.

 

Do you work on an hourly rate or quote based on the project?

I prefer to provide complete project estimates rather than working on an hourly basis, so that I can effectively manage my clients’ budget expectations and ensure that there are no surprises. There is no “one size fits all” billing solution in the design world, so I’m open to alternative arrangements, such as per hour or per project.

 

Do I have to pay upfront?

I require a deposit upon approval of the project estimates, with the remaining balance to be paid upon delivery. The deposit ensures that the client is committed to moving forward with the project. For large-scale projects, the total cost may be divided and paid upon project milestones.

 

How can I pay my invoice? What payment methods do you accept?

Invoice payments are due within 14 days of the billing date noted on the invoice. Payments are accepted by cash,

check or PayPal.
