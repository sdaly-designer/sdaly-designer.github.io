I specialize in designing strategic brands and websites—on scope, on time and on budget—and offer a wide range of creative services. I believe in open, direct communication with my clients and work closely with them to deliver exactly what they need (and sometimes even something they didn’t know they needed).

 

In addition to graphic design, I also offer consulting for new businesses facing creative challenges and for all businesses in need of social media strategy.

What I Can Do for You (My Skills & Talents)

services

1. New Business Set-up

I enjoy working with clients who are excited and passionate about a new business venture. For these clients I’ll create a complete brand package that includes: logo, business cards, print marketing materials (brochures, ads, etc.) and a website.

 

Clients of this nature typically include restaurants, fitness/nutrition ventures and independent creatives.

2. Branding & Print Design

Whether you’re a business in need of a new brand or a corporation with a well-established one, I’ll design and develop print marketing materials (brochures, ads, etc.), icons and PowerPoint templates/presentations. I’m skilled in production and will work with print vendors (yours or mine) to make sure the finished product is something you’re completely satisfied with.

3. Web Design

Though print is alive and well (contrary to what some people may think), more and more of my work has included web design. For smaller-scale sites, I typically act as both the designer and developer (meaning I create and code the entire site myself). For larger, more complex sites, it’s best to bring a developer on board to look after the programming while I focus on designing a beautiful interface.

 

I dabble in static HTML/CSS-based sites and content management systems such as WordPress and Squarespace.

4. Pro-Bono Work

I’m open to working for a good cause, especially in exchange for a little exposure. While time for unpaid projects is limited, I’ll definitely hear you out.

(Chocolate-chip cookies always help, too.)
