About Stephen & sdaly-DESIGN

For more than 10 years, I’ve provided print and web design services to a wide variety of clients, from startups to nonprofits to global corporations. I bring passion, curiosity and creativity to every project and every client, and work with companies to achieve their goals and meet their needs with a design or online experience that’s right for them. I have a BFA in graphic design, digital media and photography from the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth.

About This Website (and a Little More About My Style)

As with all design, art and technology, my website is an evolving work-in-progress. I change it, tinker with it and update it to promote different ideas and designs as necessary (or whenever I feel like it).

Many designers nowadays subscribe to the “less is more” philosophy of design/art/life. I’m completely comfortable working in this style and often have, but to be honest, it’s not my own. I’m drawn to beautiful design—not overly simple design—and prefer interesting and eye-catching to standard and bland.

If design is simple and straightforward, which it often has to be, it should have the right background or platform to elevate it, make it shine and get it noticed.
